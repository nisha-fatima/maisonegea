Shipping and Delivery

There are 3 methods of delivery:

1. Delivery by post to anywhere on the island which will cost Rs 50 and you will generally receive it the next day after being shipped. If you purchase 3 or more, delivery by post is free of charge. If delivery is done by post, we ship the next day after receiving payment.

2. In person delivery at LA City, Trianon (ex Shoprite) every Saturday which is free of charge (Time will be communicated).

3. Delivery at Plaisance Shopping Mall at Rose Belle every Sunday which is free of charge (Time will be communicated).

If you have any questions or if you are looking for your product to be shipped outside of Mauritius, please contact us at hello@inkofdeath.com, as additional shipping fees will have to be calculated.
